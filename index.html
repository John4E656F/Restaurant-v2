Meticulously crafted. Casually delicious.

From the first bite, to the last slurp – nothing about us is ordinary. We’re the experts; the game changers.

We are here for the flavor: to share the joy and excitement of umami-packed authentic ramen and Japanese soul food with you. Passed down from generations of ramen chefs from our partner’s in Japan, our recipes are unparalleled.

It’s not just about the food – with us, it’s an experience. We approach the idea of ramen and Japanese soul food with high attention to detail and efficiency: each bowl is meticulously crafted with the freshest, locally sourced ingredients, prepared in house, and served in a upbeat, casual and welcoming atmosphere.
